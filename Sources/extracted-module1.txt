GUIDE FOR SMALL GROUP I Opening Prayer (Small Group Prayer or others) Short Introductions Role Assignment: Lead Counselor, Counselee, Prayer Warrior (rotate each 20 minutes). Review of Teachings Covered: Four Basic Laws – which one affected you most? Fruit to Root – what are the fruits you are reaping now? Repentance & Restitution – is there unforgiveness in your life? Counselor starts practicing his role by encouraging counselee to share reflections or personal ‘struck’ regarding previous lessons.   Prayer Warrior prays and intercedes but also ‘in’ participation and keeping ‘time’ Counselee respond to questions /clarifications/ paraphrasing /confirming /probing /… all related to the covered teachings. Summarize the session with Counselee’s insights and understanding about the sharing of the Counselee ….leading to a prayers of Recognition and Repentance Switch Roles and end the Small Group Session with a Prayer  Encourage members of the group to meet during break time or meal time.
